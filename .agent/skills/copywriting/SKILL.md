# Copywriting

> Proven formulas for high-converting marketing copy.

---

## When to Use

| Situation | Formula |
|-----------|---------|
| Landing pages, ads | AIDA |
| Email, sales pages | PAS |
| Testimonials, case studies | BAB |
| Long-form sales | 4Ps |
| Headlines | 4Us |
| Product descriptions | FAB |

---

## Copy Formulas

### AIDA

| Step | Purpose | Example |
|------|---------|---------|
| **A**ttention | Grab interest | Bold headline |
| **I**nterest | Build curiosity | Key benefit |
| **D**esire | Create want | Social proof |
| **A**ction | Drive CTA | Clear next step |

### PAS

| Step | Purpose | Example |
|------|---------|---------|
| **P**roblem | Name the pain | "Tired of slow builds?" |
| **A**gitate | Make it worse | "Every second wasted..." |
| **S**olution | Present fix | "Introducing FastBuild" |

### BAB

| Step | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| **B**efore | Current painful state |
| **A**fter | Desired outcome |
| **B**ridge | Your solution connects them |

### FAB

| Step | Focus |
|------|-------|
| **F**eature | What it is |
| **A**dvantage | Why it matters |
| **B**enefit | How it helps you |

## Headline Templates (4Us)

| U | Question |
|---|----------|
| **U**rgent | Why act now? |
| **U**nique | What's different? |
| **U**seful | What's the benefit? |
| **U**ltra-specific | What exactly? |

**Examples:**

```markdown
❌ "Our Product is Great"
✅ "Cut Build Time by 73% in 48 Hours"

❌ "Try Our Solution"
✅ "5,000 Teams Switched This Week"
```

---

## Best Practices

| Rule | Why |
|------|-----|
| Lead with benefit | People care about outcomes |
| One CTA per piece | Reduce decision fatigue |
| Be specific | "73%" beats "faster" |
| Read aloud | Catch awkward phrasing |
| Test headlines first | Biggest impact on conversion |

---

## Anti-Patterns

| ❌ Don't | ✅ Do |
|---------|-------|
| Features first | Benefits first |
| Multiple CTAs | One clear action |
| Vague claims | Specific numbers |
| Corporate jargon | Conversational tone |
